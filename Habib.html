HABIB PUBLIC SCHOOL

Sprawled for about 18 acres, Habib Public School embodies a holistic educational system for boys. The school has many things to its credit, from its exceptional academic history to its innovative and recreational teaching methodologies to its expansive activities. In short, one could say that Habib Public School provides a systemic educational opportunity to Karachiites. The school's popularity can be attributed to its emphasis on values, a challenging curriculum, and a supportive learning environment that nurtures the potential of each student.


Admission Procedure


  • Regular intake for Class 1 and Class 11.Lateral admissions for other classes based on seat availability.
  • Separate tests for three age groups: Junior, Middle, and Senior students.
  • Social-emotional skills tested in a peer-group setting.
  • Assessment of parental values and support in a joint interview by the admin.
  • Selection is based on the clearance of the above-mentioned test and solely on merit.

      SPORTS

    The Habib Public School sprawls over nearly 18 acres of prime Karachi real estate. HPS students enjoy two swimming pools, an AstroTurf hockey field, two large sports grounds, a centrally air-conditioned gymnasium, an auditorium, and a cafeteria. The school campus is also home to spacious classrooms fitted with state-of-the-art audio-visual facilities, several science and computer labs, libraries, and projection rooms.
