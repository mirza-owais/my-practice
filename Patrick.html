St. Patrick’s High School

St. Patrick’s High School, established with a rich legacy, is celebrated for its academic rigor and commitment to holistic education. The school’s popularity is grounded in its values, diverse curriculum, and dedication to nurturing students into responsible and competent individuals. The school is the second oldest in Karachi; with being created in 1861 by Jesuits. With its deeply embedded academic heritage. Its notable alumni General Pervaz Musharraf, General Jehangir Karamat, Muhammad Khan Junejo, and Murad Ali Shah; is another feather in its cap. St Patrick High School ranks high in shaping well-rounded personalities. Furthermore, the school focuses on the Cambridge system with being best in other qualities like community service and out-performance in academics.


Admission Procedure


  • The students must qualify the admission test
  • Admissions are based on an upper and lower age limit for each class
  • Only those who have cleared their previous exams can apply
  • Quota seats available for staff and faculty members children

    • Documents Required


  • NADRA ‘B’ Form
  • Computerized NADRA Birth Certificate (Union Council handwritten Certificates will not be accepted)
  • Both Parents’ CNIC
  • NADRA FRC (mandatory)
  • Baptism Certificate (mandatory)
  • 2 recent passport size photographs of the candidate
  • For Sibling Based Forms: Report card & FRC copy of the sibling studying in St. Patrick’s high school (boys only)

    • Fee Structure


    The fee structure of St Patrick’s varies from grade to grade. However, the fee for A ‘levels is approximately 35,000 to 45,000 PKR per month, depending on the subjects. Lab charges are exclusive to it.
