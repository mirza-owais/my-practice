FOUNDATION PUBLIC SCHOOl

Foundation Public School (FPS) is a private educational institute, originated in 1981; with the sole aim of providing quality education to Karachiites. The school focuses on the Cambridge education system with its students ranking high in O & A Levels examinations. Known for its strong academic foundation and commitment to fostering critical thinking skills, the school has gained popularity among parents seeking a well-rounded education for their children. The school primarily focuses on tailoring its system to nurture its students to face the challenges of the world. Students here are equipped with recreational skills, developmental skills, critical thinking, etc.


Admission Procedure


  • Completion of the online form with the required information.
  • Confirmation will be sent through email.
  • Admission office will review forms.
  • Interview call to be expected within 15 days (if eligible).

    • Documents Required (At the time of Interview)


  • Copy of Birth Certificate. (Those made in the last six months will not be accepted)
  • Please bring along the original birth certificate (this will be returned)
  • Two (2) passport sized photographs
  • Progress report of the previous school (if any)
  • RS 1500 Application fee

    • Fee Structure


    The fee structure of FPs depends on the grade level of the students. However, O’levels Tuition fee is around 30,000-40,000 per month, varies according to the subjects the student has. For Science and ICT students Lab charges may apply.




    SPORTS


    The FPS Premier League is the hallmark of FPS. It is the intra-school, inter-house tournament in which the three houses, Jinnah, Iqbal, and Liaquat battle it out for the top prize – The Sports Trophy. The league includes a plethora of sports including football, basketball, throwball, table tennis and track and field. The finale of the Premier League is held at the Pakistan Sports Board stadium annually in the month of January or February.
