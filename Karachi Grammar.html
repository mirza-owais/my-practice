Karachi Grammar School (KGS)

Karachi Grammar School (KGS), established in 1847, is one of Pakistan's oldest and most prestigious educational institutions. Located in Karachi, it offers a rigorous British-style curriculum from Kindergarten to A-Levels, emphasizing academic excellence and holistic development. With well-equipped libraries, canteen facilities, and a wide range of sports options, KGS ensures a well-rounded education. Although it does not provide boarding, the school fosters a strong sense of community and values, producing graduates who excel both nationally and internationally.


Admission Process


KGS admission procedure is strict and fierce.

  • Entrance tests in English, Maths & General Knowledge must be cleared
  • Student’s past academic records, personality traits, and participation in other activities are also assessed.
  • Application form of PKR 5,000 must be filled by the parents.

    • Documents Required


    Interested applicants should organize the following required documents (scanned + hard copy):

  • Most recent passport-size picture of the applicant.
  • B-Form of the applicant.
  • Smart Card of the applicant.
  • NADRA Birth Certificate (not a recent extract of the same)
  • School academic report (Year 7-9) of the applicant

    • Fee Structure


    The Karachi Grammar School fee structure varies depending on the student’s grade level.

  • Kindergarten: PKR 766,800 Per Year
  • Grades 1-5: PKR 869,600 Per Year
  • Grades 6-8: PKR 1,000,800 Per Year
  • Grades 9-10: PKR 1,072,800 Per Year
  • Grades 11-12: PKR 1,245,600 Per Yea
