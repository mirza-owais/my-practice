KARACHI AMERICAN SCHOOL


Karachi American School (KAS), established in 1953, is a prestigious private international school in Karachi, Pakistan. Offering an American-style education from Pre-Kindergarten to Grade 12, KAS emphasizes a comprehensive curriculum that includes Advanced Placement (AP) courses, preparing students for higher education in the United States and globally. The school is renowned for its diverse student body, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong commitment to academic excellence, personal growth, and global citizenship. KAS fosters a supportive and inclusive community, encouraging students to achieve their highest potential both academically and personally.


Admission Process


KAS admission procedure is a bit prolonged, yet meticulous. Various factors affect admission i.e. student’s previous academic record, indulgence in extra-curricular activities, etc.

  • Online form submission.
  • Admission Fee Payment.
  • Admission Test.
  • Interview with the Admin.

    • Documents Required


  • Application Form Processing fees.
  • Completed Application Form – must be completed and signed for each child seeking admission.
  • Copy of Applicant’s birth certificate and ‘B’ Form (For Pakistan born children).
  • Copy of Applicant’s passport.
  • Copy of Father’s and Mother’s passport, including entry visa if the passport is non-Pakistani.
  • Applicant’s School Record: – Certified copies of school records.
