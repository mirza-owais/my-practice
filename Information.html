HABIB PUBLIC SCHOOL

Sprawled for about 18 acres, Habib Public School embodies a holistic educational system for boys. The school has many things to its credit, from its exceptional academic history to its innovative and recreational teaching methodologies to its expansive activities. In short, one could say that Habib Public School provides a systemic educational opportunity to Karachiites. The school's popularity can be attributed to its emphasis on values, a challenging curriculum, and a supportive learning environment that nurtures the potential of each student.


Admission Procedure


  • Regular intake for Class 1 and Class 11.Lateral admissions for other classes based on seat availability.
  • Separate tests for three age groups: Junior, Middle, and Senior students.
  • Social-emotional skills tested in a peer-group setting.
  • Assessment of parental values and support in a joint interview by the admin.
  • Selection is based on the clearance of the above-mentioned test and solely on merit.

    • Documents Required


  • Original and attested copies of the Birth Certificate & B-Form issued by NADRA.
  • 4 color photographs of the candidate (2, 1 × 1 inch and 2, passport size).
  • Original School Leaving Certificate.

    • Fee Structure


    Admission Fees Rs 35,000
    Security Deposit Rs 6,750.
    Laboratory Fees Rs 265
    Sports Fee Rs 525
    Fee Rs 12,000 (varies according to grade)

    FOUNDATION PUBLIC SCHOOl

    Foundation Public School (FPS) is a private educational institute, originated in 1981; with the sole aim of providing quality education to Karachiites. The school focuses on the Cambridge education system with its students ranking high in O & A Levels examinations. Known for its strong academic foundation and commitment to fostering critical thinking skills, the school has gained popularity among parents seeking a well-rounded education for their children. The school primarily focuses on tailoring its system to nurture its students to face the challenges of the world. Students here are equipped with recreational skills, developmental skills, critical thinking, etc.

    Karachi Grammar School (KGS)

    Karachi Grammar School (KGS), established in 1847, is one of Pakistan's oldest and most prestigious educational institutions. Located in Karachi, it offers a rigorous British-style curriculum from Kindergarten to A-Levels, emphasizing academic excellence and holistic development. With well-equipped libraries, canteen facilities, and a wide range of sports options, KGS ensures a well-rounded education. Although it does not provide boarding, the school fosters a strong sense of community and values, producing graduates who excel both nationally and internationally.


    Admission Process


    KGS admission procedure is strict and fierce.

  • Entrance tests in English, Maths & General Knowledge must be cleared
  • Student’s past academic records, personality traits, and participation in other activities are also assessed.
  • Application form of PKR 5,000 must be filled by the parents.

    • Documents Required


    Interested applicants should organize the following required documents (scanned + hard copy):

  • Most recent passport-size picture of the applicant.
  • B-Form of the applicant.
  • Smart Card of the applicant.
  • NADRA Birth Certificate (not a recent extract of the same)
  • School academic report (Year 7-9) of the applicant

    • Fee Structure


    The Karachi Grammar School fee structure varies depending on the student’s grade level.

  • Kindergarten: PKR 766,800 Per Year
  • Grades 1-5: PKR 869,600 Per Year
  • Grades 6-8: PKR 1,000,800 Per Year
  • Grades 9-10: PKR 1,072,800 Per Year
  • Grades 11-12: PKR 1,245,600 Per Yea

    KARACHI AMERICAN SCHOOL


    Karachi American School (KAS), established in 1953, is a prestigious private international school in Karachi, Pakistan. Offering an American-style education from Pre-Kindergarten to Grade 12, KAS emphasizes a comprehensive curriculum that includes Advanced Placement (AP) courses, preparing students for higher education in the United States and globally. The school is renowned for its diverse student body, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong commitment to academic excellence, personal growth, and global citizenship. KAS fosters a supportive and inclusive community, encouraging students to achieve their highest potential both academically and personally.


    Admission Process


    KAS admission procedure is a bit prolonged, yet meticulous. Various factors affect admission i.e. student’s previous academic record, indulgence in extra-curricular activities, etc.

  • Online form submission.
  • Admission Fee Payment.
  • Admission Test.
  • Interview with the Admin.

    • Documents Required


  • Application Form Processing fees.
  • Completed Application Form – must be completed and signed for each child seeking admission.
  • Copy of Applicant’s birth certificate and ‘B’ Form (For Pakistan born children).
  • Copy of Applicant’s passport.
  • Copy of Father’s and Mother’s passport, including entry visa if the passport is non-Pakistani.
  • Applicant’s School Record: – Certified copies of school records.
