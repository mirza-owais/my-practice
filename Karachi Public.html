Karachi public school



Karachi Public School (KPS) is a prominent educational institution in Karachi, Pakistan, committed to providing high-quality education from early childhood to higher secondary levels. With a focus on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth, KPS offers a nurturing and stimulating environment that prepares students for future challenges and opportunities.


Maximum Intake Capacity of Students


The specific maximum intake capacity can vary depending on the campus and grade level. KPS typically maintains a balanced student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention and quality education. For precise numbers regarding the intake capacity for different grades, it is best to contact the school administration directly.


Library Availability


KPS has well-equipped libraries across its campuses. These libraries are stocked with a diverse collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources, providing students with ample opportunities for research, reading, and learning. The libraries play a crucial role in supporting the academic and intellectual growth of students.


Canteen Facility


Karachi Public School provides canteen facilities where students can purchase a variety of healthy and nutritious meals and snacks. The canteen is designed to cater to the dietary needs of students, ensuring they have access to wholesome food options during school hours.


Boarding Availability


KPS operates as a day school and does not offer boarding facilities. Students commute to and from school daily, allowing them to spend their non-school hours with their families.



Sports Options Available


KPS places a strong emphasis on physical education and sports, offering a wide range of sports options for students. These include cricket, football, basketball, athletics, and table tennis. The school provides well-maintained sports facilities and experienced coaches, encouraging students to participate in sports activities and competitions. Engaging in sports helps students develop teamwork, leadership, and physical fitness.
