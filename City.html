City School



The City School, established in 1978, is one of Pakistan's largest private educational networks, with numerous campuses spread across the country. The school offers a rigorous curriculum based on the British education system, preparing students for O-Level and A-Level examinations. Known for its emphasis on academic excellence, holistic development, and a supportive learning environment, The City School has a strong reputation for nurturing well-rounded individuals.


Maximum Intake Capacity of Students


The intake capacity varies by campus and grade level. Each campus aims to maintain an optimal student-teacher ratio to ensure quality education and individual attention. For specific numbers, it is advisable to contact the individual campus administration directly.


Library Availability


The City School has well-equipped libraries in its campuses. These libraries offer a diverse range of books, periodicals, and digital resources, supporting both academic and extracurricular interests of students.


Canteen Facility


The school provides canteen facilities. These canteens offer a variety of healthy and nutritious food options, catering to the dietary needs of students and ensuring they have access to balanced meals during school hours.


Boarding Availability


The City School operates as a day school and does not offer boarding facilities. Students attend school during the day and return home afterward, allowing them to spend time with their families outside of school hours.



Sports Options Available


The City School offers a wide range of sports activities, promoting physical fitness and teamwork among students. Available sports typically include cricket, football, basketball, athletics, badminton, and swimming. The school provides well-maintained sports facilities and experienced coaches to guide students in their athletic endeavors.
