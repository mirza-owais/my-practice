The Smart School



The Smart School, launched by The City School network, is a modern educational institution designed to provide quality education using innovative teaching methods and technology. With a focus on academic excellence, personal development, and 21st-century skills, The Smart School aims to create a dynamic learning environment that prepares students for future challenges.


Maximum Intake Capacity of Students


The intake capacity varies by campus and grade level. Each campus strives to maintain an optimal student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention and high-quality education. For specific numbers, it is advisable to contact the school administration directly.


Library Availability


The Smart School has libraries equipped with a diverse collection of books and digital resources. These libraries support students' academic needs and foster a love for reading and research.


Canteen Facility


The school provides canteen facilities offering a variety of healthy and nutritious food options. The canteens ensure that students have access to balanced meals and snacks during school hours.


Boarding Availability


The Smart School operates as a day school and does not provide boarding facilities. Students attend school during the day and return home afterward, allowing them to spend time with their families.



Sports Options Available


The Smart School offers a range of sports activities to promote physical fitness and teamwork among students. Sports options typically include cricket, football, basketball, athletics, and badminton. The school provides adequate sports facilities and experienced coaches to guide students in their physical education and sports participation.
